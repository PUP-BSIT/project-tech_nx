ABOUT US

PAWTECHNX is a nonprofit team whose mission is to help homeless dogs, hamster, bird, rabbit and cats in the poorest communities of the Taguig City with food, shelter, medical care, adoption and humane treatment. PWATECHNX also offering to rehome the animals that we rescue for their better life.

Founded in 2024 by Alimas Eliza, Alona Pepito, Precious Pasion, Sofia Barrantes and Mikaella Tayoto after the five found an team where hundreds of animals were waiting to be euthanized by electrocution. Unable to erase the images of the suffering animals from their minds, the Five members have dedicated their lives to working with the Taguig Government, community partners and volunteers to improve the plight of homeless dogs, bird, hamster, rabbit and cats in the poorest communities of the Taguig. As a result of their tireless efforts, thousands of homeless animals have been rescued, sheltered, rehabilitated and ready to meet their soon loving families.

PAWTECHNX financially sponsors other organizations providing free spay-neuter clinics, animal rescue, rehabilitation, medical care and humane treatement and care to homeless animals in Taguig City.

VISION

PAWTECHNX is a credible non-profit team, composed of a network of committed, compassionate and trustworthy individuals and institutions that initiate and lead in the promotion of animal welfare, and the protection of animals. PAWTECHNX envisions a nation that respects animals, practices responsible pet ownership, and protects wildlife.

MISSION

PAWTECHNX exists to prevent cruelty, pain, fear and suffering in animalsand to promote a society based on humane principles.
